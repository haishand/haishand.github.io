1. P2P concepts

P2P technology is used widely around the world and many people acquire most benefit from it. It's a magic which boost the world with many sharing things.

The principle of P2P is very simple, the procedure is like below:

a. Client A and B login to Server S

b. S echo all client's external IP/Port to themselves

c. S echo A's external IP/Port to B, B's external IP/Port to A

d. A send message to B and B send message to A (just dig hole, the message will never archive its peer)

e. A and B can communicate each other
